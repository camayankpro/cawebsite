1. What Can Be Registered as a Trademark in Bhagalpur?

  • Brand names: The name of your business, product, or service
  • Logos & device marks: Your company logo, symbol, or graphical design
  • Slogans & taglines: Distinctive phrases associated with your brand
  • Combination marks: Name + logo together
  • Colour marks: Specific colour combinations distinctly associated with your brand
  • Sound marks: Distinctive jingles or sounds (e.g., startup sounds)
  • 3D shapes: Distinctive product shapes or packaging

The mark must be distinctive and not generic, descriptive, or identical/similar to an already-registered trademark. TaxAMC conducts a thorough prior-art search before filing to confirm registrability.

2. Trademark Classes — Which One Does Your Bhagalpur Business Need?

India follows the Nice Classification system with 45 trademark classes (1–34 for goods, 35–45 for services). Filing in the correct class is critical — protection is limited to the class(es) registered.

3. Documents Required for Trademark Registration in Bhagalpur

  • For individuals & proprietors: PAN card, Aadhaar card, address proof, clear logo/brand image (JPG/PNG format if registering a logo)
  • For companies & LLPs: Certificate of Incorporation, PAN of company, authorisation letter / Board Resolution, signatory's ID proof
  • For partnerships: Partnership deed, PAN of firm, authorisation letter from all partners
  • MSME/Udyam certificate (if available) — reduces government fee from ₹9,000 to ₹4,500 per class

7. Why Register Your Trademark Early?

Trademark registration in India follows the first-to-file principle — whoever files first gets the right, not necessarily who used the brand first. This makes early filing critical for Bhagalpur businesses:

  • Exclusive nationwide rights: Registered trademark gives you exclusive rights to use the mark across all of India
  • Legal action against infringers: Sue for damages, injunctions, and criminal prosecution — not possible without registration
  • Amazon Brand Registry: Required for brand protection on Amazon India marketplace
  • Business valuation: Trademarks are intangible assets that increase your company's valuation
  • Prevent domain squatting: Trademark strengthens your claim to your brand's domain and social media handles
  • Franchise and licensing: Required before you can license or franchise your brand

⚠️ Important for Bhagalpur businesses: If someone else files your brand name as a trademark before you, you may be forced to rebrand — even if you used the name for years. The cost of rebranding (new signage, packaging, marketing materials, website) far exceeds the cost of trademark registration. File early.
