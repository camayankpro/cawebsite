3. Name Change Process for LLP

  • Pass consent of all partners (or as per LLP Agreement) for the name change
  • File RUN-LLP on MCA portal for name reservation
  • File Form 5 (Notice of Change of Name of LLP) on MCA portal with partners' consent and name reservation approval
  • Amended Certificate of Incorporation issued with new name. LLPIN remains unchanged.
  • Update LLP Agreement (supplementary agreement), GST, banks, and all records

Every regulatory document â€” GST, PAN, bank, contracts â€” must reflect the new name. Our team manages all post-approval updates.
