1. What is PAN and Why it Matters in Bhuj

PAN (Permanent Account Number) is a 10-digit alphanumeric identifier issued by the Income Tax Department. It is mandatory for: filing ITR, opening bank accounts, buying or selling property above ₹10 lakh, fixed deposits above ₹50,000, GST registration, company incorporation, and most financial transactions above specified limits.

In Bhuj, with its active Kutch handicrafts (world-famous embroidery, weaving) and Salt production sectors, businesses and individuals regularly encounter PAN requirements — for vendor registrations, banking, and regulatory filings. An invalid or inoperative PAN can block critical transactions.

2. How to Apply for PAN Card Online from Bhuj

PAN applications are fully online — residents and businesses in Bhuj can apply without visiting any office:

  • NSDL (Protean): onlineservices.nsdl.com → Services → PAN → Apply Online → Form 49A (Indian citizens)
  • UTI Infrastructure: pan.utiitsl.com → Apply for New PAN

Fee: ₹107 for delivery within India (including Bhuj). PAN card delivered within 15–20 working days. e-PAN is available instantly after processing.

5. PAN Card Correction in Bhuj

If your PAN has errors — wrong name spelling, incorrect date of birth, wrong father's name — apply for correction using Form 49A on the NSDL or UTI portals. Common corrections handled by TaxAMC for Bhuj clients:

Fee: Same as new PAN application — ₹107 for domestic delivery. Supporting documents proving the correct information must be submitted.

6. PAN-Aadhaar Linking — Mandatory for Bhuj Residents

All individuals in Bhuj (except exempt categories) must link their PAN with Aadhaar. Unlinked PANs are inoperative — consequences include:

  • Cannot file ITR or receive tax refunds
  • TDS deducted at higher rate of 20% instead of standard rates
  • Cannot open new bank accounts or demat accounts
  • Critical financial transactions get blocked

How to link from Bhuj: incometax.gov.in → Link Aadhaar → Enter PAN and Aadhaar → Pay ₹1,000 penalty fee → OTP verification. Status updates within 4–7 working days.
