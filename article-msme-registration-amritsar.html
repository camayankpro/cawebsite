3. Documents Required for Udyam Registration in Amritsar

  • Mandatory: Aadhaar card of proprietor / managing partner / authorised signatory (mobile-linked for OTP verification)
  • For companies & LLPs: PAN of the company (auto-verified from ITD database)
  • Business details: Business name, type of organisation, year of establishment, business address in Amritsar, main business activity (NIC code), number of employees
  • Bank details: Account number and IFSC code
  • No physical documents required — Udyam portal auto-verifies investment and turnover data from IT returns (for PAN-linked businesses)

⚠️ Important: Provide accurate investment and turnover figures during Udyam registration. Misrepresentation or inflating figures is a punishable offence. TaxAMC's CA team ensures accurate classification based on your actual financial data.

8. Frequently Asked Questions — MSME / Udyam Registration in Amritsar

Q: Is Udyam registration free in Amritsar?
Yes. The Government of India charges no fee. The process is 100% online on the Udyam portal. TaxAMC charges a nominal professional fee for expert, error-free filing.

Q: What is the investment limit for MSME classification?
Micro: investment up to ₹1 crore & turnover up to ₹5 crore. Small: investment up to ₹10 crore & turnover up to ₹50 crore. Medium: investment up to ₹50 crore & turnover up to ₹250 crore.

Q: Can a service business in Amritsar get MSME registration?
Yes. Both manufacturing and service businesses are equally eligible. Professionals, traders, retailers, and service providers in Amritsar can all register.

Q: Does Udyam registration need to be renewed?
No. Udyam registration has lifetime validity. However, you must update your turnover and investment data annually on the portal when they change.

Q: Can I get a loan with Udyam registration in Amritsar?
Yes. MSME-registered businesses are eligible for collateral-free loans up to ₹2 crore under CGTMSE, and priority lending from all scheduled commercial banks at preferential rates.
