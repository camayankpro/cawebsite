3. Documents Required for FSSAI

  • Photo ID and address proof of proprietor / partner / director
  • Proof of business address (rent agreement + NOC, utility bill, or ownership document)
  • List of food products to be manufactured / sold
  • Blueprint / layout of food premises (for State / Central License)
  • List of equipment and machinery (for manufacturing licenses)
  • NOC from municipality / local body / panchayat (as applicable)
  • For State / Central License: Water testing report, medical fitness certificate of food handlers
