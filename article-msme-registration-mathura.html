3. Documents Required for Udyam Registration in Mathura

  • Mandatory: Aadhaar card of proprietor / managing partner / authorised signatory (mobile-linked for OTP verification)
  • For companies & LLPs: PAN of the company (auto-verified from ITD database)
  • Business details: Business name, type of organisation, year of establishment, business address in Mathura, main business activity (NIC code), number of employees
  • Bank details: Account number and IFSC code
  • No physical documents required — Udyam portal auto-verifies investment and turnover data from IT returns (for PAN-linked businesses)

⚠️ Important: Provide accurate investment and turnover figures during Udyam registration. Misrepresentation or inflating figures is a punishable offence. TaxAMC's CA team ensures accurate classification based on your actual financial data.

7. Key Government Schemes for MSMEs in Mathura

  • CGTMSE (Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for MSMEs): Collateral-free loans up to ₹2 crore — the single most valuable benefit for small businesses in Mathura
  • PMEGP (Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme): Subsidy of 15–35% on project cost for new enterprises
  • MUDRA Loans: Shishu (up to ₹50,000), Kishore (up to ₹5 lakh), Tarun (up to ₹10 lakh) for micro businesses
  • GeM (Government e-Marketplace): Sell directly to central and state government departments — over ₹2 lakh crore in annual procurement
  • ZED Certification: Zero Defect Zero Effect quality certification with subsidy for MSME manufacturers
  • Uttar Pradesh MSME Policy: State-level subsidies on land, electricity, and capital investment for manufacturing MSMEs — available in Mathura
