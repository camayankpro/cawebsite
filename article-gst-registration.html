3. Documents Required for GST Registration

  • PAN of the business / applicant
  • Aadhaar of proprietor / partners / directors (Aadhaar-linked mobile required for OTP)
  • Proof of business registration (Partnership Deed / MOA / LLP Agreement / Udyam certificate)
  • Address proof of principal place of business: electricity bill, rent agreement + NOC from landlord, or ownership document
  • Bank account details: cancelled cheque or bank statement
  • Photograph of proprietor / authorised signatory
  • For companies/LLPs: Certificate of Incorporation
