1. Business ITR Overview for Visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam's business landscape — Steel production (Vizag Steel), Petrochemicals (HPCL refinery), IT and ITES (Rushikonda) — means businesses here have diverse tax profiles. Key decisions before filing ITR for FY 2025-26:

  • Do you qualify for presumptive taxation under Section 44AD or 44ADA?
  • Is your turnover above the tax audit threshold (₹1 crore for businesses)?
  • Are you maintaining proper books of accounts?
  • Have you paid advance tax in four instalments?
  • Does your ITR turnover match your GST annual return?

2. ITR-3 vs ITR-4 for Visakhapatnam Business Owners

FormWho Should Use
ITR-4 (Sugam)Businesses in Visakhapatnam opting for presumptive taxation — turnover up to ₹2 crore (Section 44AD) or professional receipts up to ₹75 lakh (Section 44ADA) — with no capital gains or foreign assets
ITR-3Regular accounting — turnover above audit limits, businesses with capital gains, those not eligible for presumptive taxation, or those maintaining full books

3. Presumptive Taxation Under Section 44AD for Visakhapatnam Businesses

Small traders and manufacturers in Visakhapatnam with turnover up to ₹2 crore can opt for Section 44AD presumptive taxation — declaring 8% of gross turnover as income (6% for fully digital receipts) without maintaining detailed books. This is ideal for small businesses in Visakhapatnam's Steel production (Vizag Steel) sector that want to minimise compliance burden.

Section 44ADA for professionals: Doctors, lawyers, engineers, architects, and consultants in Visakhapatnam with gross receipts up to ₹75 lakh can declare 50% as presumptive income under 44ADA.

Important restriction: If you opt out of presumptive in any year, you cannot use it for the next 5 years. Our CA team evaluates whether presumptive is sustainable for your Visakhapatnam business before recommending it.
