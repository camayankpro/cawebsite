2. GST Registration & Returns for Pilkhuwa Industrial Area Businesses

All businesses in Pilkhuwa Industrial Area with annual turnover above ₹40 lakh (₹20 lakh for service providers) must register for GST. Manufacturing units making inter-state supplies must register regardless of turnover. Key GST services for Pilkhuwa Industrial Area businesses:

  • New GST Registration — complete documentation and portal filing
  • Monthly GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B — accurate outward supply reporting and tax payment
  • Annual GSTR-9 & GSTR-9C — reconciliation and audit certification
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) Management — maximise ITC on raw materials, machinery, and services
  • E-way Bill Compliance — for goods movement within and outside Uttar Pradesh
  • Export LUT Filing — for businesses exporting from Pilkhuwa Industrial Area without paying IGST
  • GST Notice Handling — respond to scrutiny and demand notices

Businesses in Pilkhuwa Industrial Area deal with regular inter-state and intra-state transactions requiring timely GST compliance.

5. TDS Compliance for Pilkhuwa Industrial Area Businesses

Businesses in Pilkhuwa Industrial Area making payments to contractors, workers, or professionals must deduct TDS. Key TDS obligations for industrial businesses:

Payment TypeSectionTDS RateThreshold
Contractors & sub-contractors194C1% (individuals) / 2% (others)₹30,000 per payment or ₹1 lakh per year
Professional fees194J10%₹30,000 per year
Rent for factory / land194I10%₹2.4 lakh per year
Purchase of goods194Q0.1%Purchases above ₹50 lakh/year from a single supplier
Salary to employees192As per slabAbove basic exemption

TDS compliance calendar for Pilkhuwa Industrial Area businesses: TDS must be deposited by the 7th of the following month. Quarterly TDS returns (24Q for salary, 26Q for others) must be filed on time. Failure attracts 1.5% per month interest and disallowance of the expense in ITR.
