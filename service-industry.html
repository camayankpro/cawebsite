E-commerce Compliance — What Sellers Must Know

  • All sellers on Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho etc. must be GST registered — regardless of turnover
  • Marketplace collects Tax Collected at Source (TCS) at 1% and deposits on your behalf
  • TCS credit is reflected in your GSTR-2B and can be claimed as ITC
  • E-commerce operators (platforms) must file GSTR-8 monthly
  • Reconciliation between marketplace settlement reports and GST returns is critical
