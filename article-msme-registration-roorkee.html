3. Documents Required for Udyam Registration in Roorkee

  • Mandatory: Aadhaar card of proprietor / managing partner / authorised signatory (mobile-linked for OTP verification)
  • For companies & LLPs: PAN of the company (auto-verified from ITD database)
  • Business details: Business name, type of organisation, year of establishment, business address in Roorkee, main business activity (NIC code), number of employees
  • Bank details: Account number and IFSC code
  • No physical documents required — Udyam portal auto-verifies investment and turnover data from IT returns (for PAN-linked businesses)
