1. What Can Be Registered as a Trademark in Aurangabad?

  • Brand names: The name of your business, product, or service
  • Logos & device marks: Your company logo, symbol, or graphical design
  • Slogans & taglines: Distinctive phrases associated with your brand
  • Combination marks: Name + logo together
  • Colour marks: Specific colour combinations distinctly associated with your brand
  • Sound marks: Distinctive jingles or sounds (e.g., startup sounds)
  • 3D shapes: Distinctive product shapes or packaging

The mark must be distinctive and not generic, descriptive, or identical/similar to an already-registered trademark. TaxAMC conducts a thorough prior-art search before filing to confirm registrability.

2. Trademark Classes — Which One Does Your Aurangabad Business Need?

India follows the Nice Classification system with 45 trademark classes (1–34 for goods, 35–45 for services). Filing in the correct class is critical — protection is limited to the class(es) registered.

3. Documents Required for Trademark Registration in Aurangabad

  • For individuals & proprietors: PAN card, Aadhaar card, address proof, clear logo/brand image (JPG/PNG format if registering a logo)
  • For companies & LLPs: Certificate of Incorporation, PAN of company, authorisation letter / Board Resolution, signatory's ID proof
  • For partnerships: Partnership deed, PAN of firm, authorisation letter from all partners
  • MSME/Udyam certificate (if available) — reduces government fee from ₹9,000 to ₹4,500 per class

4. Trademark Registration Process for Aurangabad Businesses

📝 100% online — managed entirely by our CA team. From search to certificate, we handle everything. You use the ™ symbol from Day 1 of filing.

  • Step 1 — Prior-Art Search: We search the trademark registry to confirm your mark is available and registrable
  • Step 2 — Class Selection: CA advises on correct Nice Classification class(es) for your business
  • Step 3 — Application Filing: Form TM-A filed on the IP India portal with supporting documents
  • Step 4 — TM Number Issued: Application number issued within 1–2 working days — you can use ™ symbol immediately
  • Step 5 — Examination: Trademark examiner reviews the application (typically 3–6 months)
  • Step 6 — Objection Handling: If examination report raises objections, TaxAMC files a detailed reply on your behalf
  • Step 7 — Publication & Opposition: Mark published in Trademark Journal for 4-month opposition window

7. Why Register Your Trademark Early?

Trademark registration in India follows the first-to-file principle — whoever files first gets the right, not necessarily who used the brand first. This makes early filing critical for Aurangabad businesses:

  • Exclusive nationwide rights: Registered trademark gives you exclusive rights to use the mark across all of India
  • Legal action against infringers: Sue for damages, injunctions, and criminal prosecution — not possible without registration
  • Amazon Brand Registry: Required for brand protection on Amazon India marketplace
  • Business valuation: Trademarks are intangible assets that increase your company's valuation
  • Prevent domain squatting: Trademark strengthens your claim to your brand's domain and social media handles
  • Franchise and licensing: Required before you can license or franchise your brand

⚠️ Important for Aurangabad businesses: If someone else files your brand name as a trademark before you, you may be forced to rebrand — even if you used the name for years. The cost of rebranding (new signage, packaging, marketing materials, website) far exceeds the cost of trademark registration. File early.

You get a TM application number within 1–2 days and can use the ™ symbol immediately. Full ® registration takes 18–24 months, subject to examination and opposition.

Q: What is the government fee for trademark registration?
₹4,500 per class for individuals, startups, and MSME-registered businesses. ₹9,000 per class for companies and other entities. TaxAMC's professional fee covers search, filing, and follow-up.

Q: Can I register my logo as a trademark in Aurangabad?
Yes. Logos, brand names, slogans, and combination marks can all be registered. We conduct a prior-art search to confirm availability before filing.

Q: What if someone objects to my trademark application?
