2. GST Registration & Returns for Ludhiana Industrial Area Businesses

All businesses in Ludhiana Industrial Area with annual turnover above ₹40 lakh (₹20 lakh for service providers) must register for GST. Manufacturing units making inter-state supplies must register regardless of turnover. Key GST services for Ludhiana Industrial Area businesses:

  • New GST Registration — complete documentation and portal filing
  • Monthly GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B — accurate outward supply reporting and tax payment
  • Annual GSTR-9 & GSTR-9C — reconciliation and audit certification
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) Management — maximise ITC on raw materials, machinery, and services
  • E-way Bill Compliance — for goods movement within and outside Punjab
  • Export LUT Filing — for businesses exporting from Ludhiana Industrial Area without paying IGST
  • GST Notice Handling — respond to scrutiny and demand notices

Businesses in Ludhiana Industrial Area deal with regular inter-state and intra-state transactions requiring timely GST compliance.

3. Income Tax & ITR Filing for Ludhiana Industrial Area Business Owners

Business owners in Ludhiana Industrial Area must file annual ITR — the form and complexity depending on business structure and turnover:

Business TypeITR FormDeadline
Proprietorship (below ₹2 crore, presumptive)ITR-431st July 2026
Proprietorship (regular accounting)ITR-331st July / 31st October
Partnership Firm / LLPITR-531st October 2026 (if audit)
Private Limited CompanyITR-631st October 2026

For businesses in Ludhiana Industrial Area with turnover above ₹1 crore, a Tax Audit under Section 44AB is mandatory — conducted by a CA and filed with Form 3CA/3CB and 3CD. TaxAMC handles complete tax audit and ITR filing for Ludhiana Industrial Area businesses.

Key deductions for Ludhiana Industrial Area manufacturers: Depreciation on plant and machinery (Section 32), interest on business loans, raw material costs, labour and contractor payments, factory rent, and R&D expenditure under Section 35.

5. TDS Compliance for Ludhiana Industrial Area Businesses

Businesses in Ludhiana Industrial Area making payments to contractors, workers, or professionals must deduct TDS. Key TDS obligations for industrial businesses:

Payment TypeSectionTDS RateThreshold
Contractors & sub-contractors194C1% (individuals) / 2% (others)₹30,000 per payment or ₹1 lakh per year
Professional fees194J10%₹30,000 per year
Rent for factory / land194I10%₹2.4 lakh per year
Purchase of goods194Q0.1%Purchases above ₹50 lakh/year from a single supplier
Salary to employees192As per slabAbove basic exemption

TDS compliance calendar for Ludhiana Industrial Area businesses: TDS must be deposited by the 7th of the following month. Quarterly TDS returns (24Q for salary, 26Q for others) must be filed on time. Failure attracts 1.5% per month interest and disallowance of the expense in ITR.
