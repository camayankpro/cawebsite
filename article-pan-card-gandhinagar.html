1. What is PAN and Why it Matters in Gandhinagar

PAN (Permanent Account Number) is a 10-digit alphanumeric identifier issued by the Income Tax Department. It is mandatory for: filing ITR, opening bank accounts, buying or selling property above ₹10 lakh, fixed deposits above ₹50,000, GST registration, company incorporation, and most financial transactions above specified limits.

In Gandhinagar, with its active GIFT City financial services (IFSC) and Government and administration sectors, businesses and individuals regularly encounter PAN requirements — for vendor registrations, banking, and regulatory filings. An invalid or inoperative PAN can block critical transactions.

2. How to Apply for PAN Card Online from Gandhinagar

PAN applications are fully online — residents and businesses in Gandhinagar can apply without visiting any office:

  • NSDL (Protean): onlineservices.nsdl.com → Services → PAN → Apply Online → Form 49A (Indian citizens)
  • UTI Infrastructure: pan.utiitsl.com → Apply for New PAN

Fee: ₹107 for delivery within India (including Gandhinagar). PAN card delivered within 15–20 working days. e-PAN is available instantly after processing.

6. PAN-Aadhaar Linking — Mandatory for Gandhinagar Residents

All individuals in Gandhinagar (except exempt categories) must link their PAN with Aadhaar. Unlinked PANs are inoperative — consequences include:

  • Cannot file ITR or receive tax refunds
  • TDS deducted at higher rate of 20% instead of standard rates
  • Cannot open new bank accounts or demat accounts
  • Critical financial transactions get blocked

How to link from Gandhinagar: incometax.gov.in → Link Aadhaar → Enter PAN and Aadhaar → Pay ₹1,000 penalty fee → OTP verification. Status updates within 4–7 working days.
